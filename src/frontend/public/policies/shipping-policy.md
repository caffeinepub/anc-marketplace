# Shipping Policy

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

## Overview

This Shipping Policy outlines the terms and conditions for shipping products purchased through ANC Electronics N More and ANC Marketplace.

## Shipping Methods

We offer various shipping methods depending on your location and the products ordered:
- Standard Shipping (5-7 business days)
- Expedited Shipping (2-3 business days)
- Express Shipping (1-2 business days)

## Shipping Costs

Shipping costs are calculated based on:
- Package weight and dimensions
- Destination address
- Selected shipping method

Shipping costs will be displayed at checkout before you complete your purchase.

## Processing Time

Orders are typically processed within 1-2 business days. Processing time does not include shipping time.

## Tracking Information

Once your order ships, you will receive a confirmation email with tracking information. You can track your package using the provided tracking number.

## Delivery

Delivery times are estimates and not guaranteed. We are not responsible for delays caused by:
- Weather conditions
- Carrier delays
- Incorrect shipping addresses
- Customs processing (for international orders)

## International Shipping

We ship to select international locations. International orders may be subject to:
- Customs duties and taxes
- Import fees
- Extended delivery times

Customers are responsible for all customs fees and import taxes.

## Shipping Restrictions

Some products may have shipping restrictions based on:
- Destination location
- Product type
- Legal regulations

## Address Changes

If you need to change your shipping address, please contact us immediately at support@anc-electronics-n-services.net. We cannot guarantee address changes once an order has shipped.

## Lost or Damaged Packages

If your package is lost or damaged during shipping:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of damaged items
3. Include your order number and tracking information

We will work with you to resolve the issue promptly.
