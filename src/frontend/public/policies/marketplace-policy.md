## Introduction

Welcome to ANC Marketplace. This Marketplace Policy governs your use of our platform and outlines the terms and conditions applicable to all users, including customers, sellers, and businesses.

## Marketplace Overview

ANC Marketplace operates as a digital marketplace connecting buyers and sellers. We provide the platform infrastructure while independent merchants (such as ANC Electronics N Services, specializing in business and industrial products) manage their own products, services, and customer relationships.

- You must fulfill orders in a timely manner according to your stated policies
- You retain all copyright and intellectual property rights to your products
- You are responsible for customer service related to your products
- You must maintain appropriate business licenses and permits

### For Customers

- You agree to provide accurate shipping and payment information
- You are responsible for reviewing seller policies before making purchases
- You agree to contact sellers directly for product-specific inquiries

## Service Fees

- Marketplace access is free for all users
- A $5 service fee applies to each completed sale
- Optional Store Builder subscription available at $10/month for standalone website/app conversion
- All fees are clearly disclosed before transactions are completed

## Payment Processing

- All payments are processed through secure third-party payment providers
- ANC Marketplace temporarily holds seller funds in an internal ledger until they are transferred to the seller's designated payout account
- Sellers receive payments according to their configured payout preferences
- Service fees are automatically deducted from transactions

## Dispute Resolution

- ANC may assist in facilitating communication between parties
