# Returns Policy

**Last Updated: February 13, 2026**

## Return Eligibility

We want you to be completely satisfied with your purchase. Items may be returned within 30 days of delivery if they meet the following conditions:
- Item is unused and in original condition
- Original packaging is intact
- All accessories and documentation are included
- Proof of purchase is provided

## Non-Returnable Items

The following items cannot be returned:
- Digital products and downloads
- Personalized or custom-made items
- Perishable goods
- Items marked as final sale
- Gift cards

## Return Process

To initiate a return:
1. Contact us at support@anc-electronics-n-services.net within 30 days of delivery
2. Provide your order number and reason for return
3. Wait for return authorization and instructions
4. Ship the item back using a trackable shipping method
5. Include the return authorization number with your package

## Return Shipping Costs

- Defective or incorrect items: We cover return shipping costs
- Change of mind or buyer's remorse: Customer is responsible for return shipping costs

## Refund Processing

Once we receive and inspect your returned item:
- Approved refunds are processed within 5-7 business days
- Refunds are issued to the original payment method
- Original shipping costs are non-refundable (except for defective items)

## Exchanges

We do not offer direct exchanges. If you need a different item:
1. Return the original item for a refund
2. Place a new order for the desired item

## Damaged or Defective Items

If you receive a damaged or defective item:
1. Contact us within 48 hours of delivery
2. Provide photos of the damage or defect
3. Include your order number
4. We will arrange for a replacement or full refund

## Late or Missing Refunds

If you haven't received your refund after 7 business days:
1. Check your bank account or credit card statement
2. Contact your credit card company (processing may take time)
3. Contact your bank
4. If you've done all of this and still haven't received your refund, contact us at support@anc-electronics-n-services.net

## Restocking Fees

A restocking fee of 15% may apply to:
- Returns without original packaging
- Items showing signs of use
- Returns after 30 days (at our discretion)
